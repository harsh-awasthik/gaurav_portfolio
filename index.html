Gaurav Awashti


Artistic Statement

Gaurav Awasthi is a photographer based in Delhi, India. He graduated with a bachelor's degree in Commerce but left the field to pursue a photography career. Since then, he has been documenting social, political, and inner societal conflicts through his photography. In 2024, he obtained his photography and fine art diploma from the Indian Institute of Photography. He utilizes analog photography and experimental techniques to provide a visual narrative to his storytelling process.
