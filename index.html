Gaurav Awashti

Artistic Statement

Gaurav Awasthi is a photographer based in Delhi, India. He graduated with a bachelor's degree in Commerce but left the field to pursue a photography career. Since then, he has been documenting social, political, and inner societal conflicts through his photography. In 2024, he obtained his photography and fine art diploma from the Indian Institute of Photography. He utilizes analog photography and experimental techniques to provide a visual narrative to his storytelling process. Description of work  Title of Project: Internal Conversation Year: 2023Medium: Photography The images enclosed in this email are part of my project, "Internal Conversation." As time progresses, we tend to detach from certain things and become attached to others. Similarly, as I transitioned from childhood to adulthood and shouldered responsibilities, I often found myself feeling isolated and nostalgic for the familiarity of the past. To escape from this reality, I frequently engage in conversations with my memories. In my artistic approach, I utilized self-portraiture, drawing inspiration from its historical significance and popularity from the Early Renaissance. Through the use of double exposure, combining self-portraits and still-life elements, I aimed to evoke a sense of ambiguity between memories and reality, fostering a thought-provoking experience.
